Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 17, 2025

Encinitas Trailer Rental respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Contact details such as name, phone number, email, and billing address that you provide when you book or contact us.
  • Booking and payment information needed to provide our services. Payments may be processed by a third party.
  • Communications that you send to us, including emails or website form submissions.
  • Website usage information collected through cookies or similar technologies. See Cookies below.

How we use information

  • Provide, operate, and improve our rental services.
  • Communicate with you about bookings, support, and updates.
  • Process payments and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service providers that help us run our business such as payment processors, scheduling tools, and website hosting. They may use information only to perform services for us.
  • Authorities when required by law or to protect our rights.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to make the site work. We may use basic analytics cookies to understand site traffic. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes above and as required by law.

Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information.
  • You can opt out of non essential emails at any time.

California residents

If you live in California, you have rights under state law to request access to or deletion of personal information and to know whether we sell or share personal information. We do not sell personal information. To make a request, use the contact information below.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new date when we do.
